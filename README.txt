# Controls   #
##############

1. Play Mode
Click two tiles in succession to initiate a swap. Tiles will disappear once they match with 3 or more of the same tiles

2. Legacy Mode
I made this mode to learn about the Unity Engine. It's a little buggy but it was indeed my first foray into game development

	Left Arrow Key - Move Left
	Right Arrow Key - Move Right
	Right Click - Move Up
	Space Bar - Move Down


	############
	# Goal (4) #
	############

	Gain points by either colliding with the North or South parts of the board.

	Keep the gem within the board without succumbing to any falls on the East and West flanks.

	If you hit any enemy gems, the game will restart and you will lose all your points

	Upon approaching the East or West flanks of the board, the game will reset.


	###############
	Sound Controls
	###############

	Press 'M' to toggle the background sound (Only in Legacy Mode)
